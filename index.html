鴨本身不是一個很喜歡社交的一個人，但鴨很常去將一些課外的活動來充實我的空閒時間。主要有三維建模啊，模聯啊， 寫作啊，(適量的)遊戲等等。與此同時，鴨也是一個非常熱愛學習的人，不僅從閱讀小說中學習，也會適度地在社會學、 心理學、地緣政治、歷史等方面都有一些研究。鴨也會將微國當作一個介於興趣和兼職工作之間的東西(鴨是沒錢拿的)， 主要是在沒有模聯的時候給自己磨練磨練。鴨對學習的熱衷強得有點可怕，有時候更是會停不下來；鴨也在其中一個微國 創建了一個知識分享平台，雖然聽起來會覺得鴨有點瘋狂，但鴨是很享受這個過程的。
I'm not a very social person myself, but I do like to fill my extra time with some activities I enjoy. It varies from 3D Modelling, Model UN Conferences, writing, (an acceptable amount of interest in gaming) and so on. In the same time, I'm a crazily enthusiastic learner, not only from reading fictional novels, but also moderate studies from sociology, psychology, geopolitics, history, and so on. I also work on micronations as something between a hobby and a part-time job, though I seriously do not get paid to do so, just to sharpen my skills. Trying to learn as much as I can, and to be honest, I don't know how to stop myself from doing so, I even started a knowledge sharing platform on one of the micronations I work for. It may sound like I'm insane, but I enjoy it.
