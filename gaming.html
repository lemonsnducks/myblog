Delivery Not Guaranteed

Delivery not Guaranteed是一個改編Tradelands的Roblox遊戲，但改成了飛艇和空中船艦的版本，這引起了鴨的注意。由於額外的高度 特性和海盜，讓這個遊戲變得更加冒險和有趣。鴨已經玩了一段很長的時間了，必須說，它是鴨在Roblox上最喜歡的遊戲之一。
這是我從這個遊戲中拍攝得最好的風景照。
Delivery Not Guaranteed is a Roblox game as a remix of Tradelands, but in a blimp and airship version, which definitely caught my attention. It's a lot more risky and interesting due to the additional altitude feature and pirates, and I really enjoy it. I have been playing it for a while, and I have to say, it's one of the games I enjoy the most on Roblox.
